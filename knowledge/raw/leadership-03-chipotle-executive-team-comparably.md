# Chipotle Executive Team

The Chipotle Executive Team is rated a "B" and led by CEO Scott Boatwright. Chipotle employees rate their Executive Team in the Top 25% of similar size companies on Comparably with 10,000+ Employees.

The Communications department and the HR department are more confident in their Executive Team, while the Customer Success department and Non-Binary employees think there is room for improvement. Out of their 2 competitors, Sweetgreen and McDonald's, Chipotle's score ranks in 2nd place.

### Chipotle Executive Team At a Glance

The Executive Team Score of 72/100 is calculated by combining aggregate responses to the questions of “How would you rate your executive team?” and “Do you approve of the job your executive team is doing at your company?”

70% of employees say “Yes, they approve of the job the executive team is doing at Chipotle”, with 100% of the Marketing department saying yes and 86% of employees with Over 10 Years experience saying yes.

The last rating scoring Chipotle's executive team was provided months ago and has remained stable at 72 over the last 90 days.
